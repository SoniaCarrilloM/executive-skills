EXECUTIVE SKILLS

Executive functions (EFs) largely determine your academic success, perhaps even more than intelligence. This is because EFs assist in exhibiting goal-directed behavior. You can be intelligent, but if you are distracted by every stimulus or every internal stimulus, then learning becomes more difficult.
