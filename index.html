EXECUTIEVE VAARDIGHEDEN

Executive functions (EFs) largely determine your academic success, perhaps even more than intelligence. This is because EFs assist in exhibiting goal-directed behavior. You can be intelligent, but if you are distracted by every stimulus or every internal stimulus, then learning becomes more difficult. Types of Executive Skills:
  • Starting tasks
  • Planning and Prioritizing
  • Attention Span and Focus
  • Emotion Regulations
  • Working Memory
  • Inhibition (self-control)
  • Self-awareness
  • Cognitive Flexibility
